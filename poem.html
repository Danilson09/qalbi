Qalbi Poem

Your eyes, Your eyes, Your beautiful eyes
9 months together, I still get butterflies
I feel them in my stomach and my heart
They are truly a marvellous work of art
Because of your eyes, in God I believe
The visual embodiment of Christmas Eve
So pretty, I am hypnotised
9 months later, I am still surprised
From Malta to Poland, with the distance I am trying to cope
But reminiscing about your eyes, I get filled with hope
Your eyes, Your eyes, Your beautiful eyes
9 months together, I still get butterflies
