A Letter to My Love

Dear Daria,

I have made you this website not because you where mad about the letter, but because I want to make something special for us. This website will be a place where we can forever store our pictures and videos and access them from anywhere! Anyways, I love you Daria, you have been the best thing that happened to me in the past year or so, having a girlfriend like you beats the fact that I graduated, because I can always try again next year but i can never find someone as special as you. You mean the world to me qalbi. For the past week I have been looking at our photos and at first I get sad, but then I remember and get hopeful that one day I will see you again, you actually made me try long distance I cannot believe this. I love everything about you, your looks, BEAUTIFUL, your character, somehow even better because my god youre so kind, sweet, loving, smart, funny, LOYAL and I like that you know. Youre one of the best human beings I know and certainly my favourite.

Love,
Danilson Zammit
